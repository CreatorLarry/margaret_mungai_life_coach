From Stuck to Unstoppable

Hey, my name is Margaret Mungai. I'm a Transformational Life Coach, Trainer, Speaker, and the founder of The Unstuck Woman Program. I help individuals and teams who feel stuck, unseen, and disconnected from their purpose to reclaim their voice, rebuild their identity, and create a life rooted in clarity, confidence, and freedom.

My approach blends science, psychology, spirituality, and soulful self-awareness to support clients through identity shifts, emotional healing, goal-setting, self-awareness, self-mastery, and the release of limiting beliefs. Whether you're navigating burnout, going through a major life transition, feeling stuck in a loop, or simply living a life that no longer feels like your own — my coaching offers the safety, structure, and support you need to reset, heal, and rise.
